Privacy Policy

Your trust matters. Learn how we collect, use, and safeguard your information.

Last updated: April 1, 2026

1. Introduction

Kotak & Associates and A & B Business Advisory LLP are committed to protecting the privacy of our clients, website visitors, and all individuals who interact with our services. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and safeguard your personal information when you use our website or engage our Virtual CFO and financial advisory services.

By accessing our website or using our services, you agree to the terms outlined in this Privacy Policy. We encourage you to read this document carefully to understand our practices regarding your personal data.

2. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information when you interact with us:

  • Full name and designation
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Company or business name
  • Business address
  • Financial data and documents shared during consultation, including but not limited to balance sheets, profit and loss statements, tax records, and bank statements
  • GST and PAN details, where applicable
  • Any other information you voluntarily provide during the course of our engagement

3. How We Use Your Information

The information we collect is used for the following purposes:

  • Providing Virtual CFO, financial advisory, and consulting services as agreed upon in our engagement
  • Communicating with you regarding your account, services, updates, and inquiries
  • Preparing financial reports, analyses, and recommendations tailored to your business
  • Improving the quality and scope of our services
  • Complying with legal and regulatory requirements applicable to our practice
  • Sending periodic updates or newsletters, only with your consent

4. Data Security

We take the security of your data seriously and implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your information from unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. These measures include:

  • SSL encryption for all data transmitted through our website
  • Secure servers with restricted access hosted by reputable service providers
  • Access controls that limit data access to authorized personnel only
  • Regular review of our security practices and procedures
  • Secure handling and storage of physical documents, where applicable

While we strive to protect your personal information, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is completely secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security, but we are committed to maintaining the highest practicable standards.

5. Third-Party Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share your information in the following limited circumstances:

  • With authorized service providers who assist us in delivering our services, such as cloud hosting providers and accounting software platforms, under strict confidentiality agreements
  • When required by law, regulation, or legal process
  • To protect the rights, property, or safety of Kotak & Associates, our clients, or others
  • With your explicit consent

6. Cookies

Our website uses cookies to enhance your browsing experience and to collect analytical data about website usage. Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit our website.

We use cookies for the following purposes:

  • Analytics - to understand how visitors interact with our website and to improve its performance
  • User experience - to remember your preferences and provide a personalized experience
  • Functionality - to ensure the website operates correctly

You can control or disable cookies through your browser settings. However, disabling cookies may affect certain features of our website.

7. Your Rights

You have the following rights regarding your personal information:

  • Access - You may request a copy of the personal data we hold about you
  • Correction - You may request that we correct any inaccurate or incomplete information
  • Deletion - You may request that we delete your personal data, subject to any legal obligations requiring us to retain certain records
  • Objection - You may object to certain types of processing, such as marketing communications
  • Portability - You may request your data in a structured, commonly used format

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us using the details provided below. We will respond to your request within a reasonable timeframe.

8. Data Retention

We retain your personal information only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes for which it was collected, including satisfying any legal, accounting, or reporting requirements.

In general, client engagement data is retained for a period consistent with applicable laws and professional standards. Financial records and related documents are typically retained for a minimum of eight years following the completion of the engagement, as required under Indian tax and corporate laws.

Once the retention period expires, your data will be securely deleted or anonymized.

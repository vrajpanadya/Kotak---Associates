Our Mission

At Kotak & Associates & A & B Business Advisory LLP, our mission is to provide expert Virtual CFO services to businesses, helping them achieve financial clarity, growth, and success.

Our team of experienced finance professionals offers a range of services, including financial planning, budgeting, forecasting, and financial reporting. We take a client-centric approach, working closely with business owners to understand their unique needs and goals.

Do You Know, Entrepreneur?

Click on any question to discover how we can help

Whether your financial complexities are holding you back? +

Many businesses struggle with tangled finances — unclear reports, messy books, and no clarity on where money is going. Our Virtual CFO service streamlines your financial systems, giving you a clear picture so you can focus on growing your business without financial confusion.

Whether your business is prepared for unexpected financial challenges or risks? +

Most businesses don't plan for financial emergencies until it's too late. We help you build a strong financial safety net through risk assessment, contingency planning, and smart reserve management — so your business stays resilient no matter what comes.

Whether your financial stress impacts your business growth decisions? +

When finances are uncertain, business owners hesitate to invest, expand, or hire. We remove that stress by providing data-driven financial insights and forecasts, empowering you to make confident growth decisions backed by real numbers.

Where your business stands compared to your competitors? +

Without benchmarking, you're flying blind. We analyze your financial performance against industry standards and competitors, identifying gaps and opportunities so you can stay ahead in your market.

Whether your financial reports are accurate and providing insights for informed decisions? +

Inaccurate or delayed reports lead to poor decisions. We ensure your financial reporting is timely, accurate, and packed with actionable insights — turning raw numbers into a strategic roadmap for your business.

Whether you are struggling to manage your company's cash flow? +

Cash flow is the lifeline of any business. We implement robust cash flow management systems — tracking receivables, optimizing payables, and forecasting future cash needs — so you never face a cash crunch again.

Whether your business is overspending and where you can save money? +

Hidden costs and unnecessary expenses eat into your profits silently. Our detailed cost analysis identifies exactly where you're overspending and provides actionable strategies to cut costs without compromising quality or growth.

We can provide you a strategic financial partner without the cost of a full-time CFO.

Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our engagement process

How long does the full onboarding process take?
Typical onboarding through the goal-setting stage takes 3-4 weeks. You'll see your first tangible results within 30 days of engagement, with full process maturity achieved by month 3.
Do we need to change our existing accounting software?
Not at all! We work seamlessly with Tally, Zoho Books, QuickBooks, Busy, and most major platforms. We build our dashboards and reporting layer on top of whatever you already use.
Will our existing finance team be replaced?
Absolutely not. We augment and empower your team. Step 5 (Training) is specifically designed to upskill your in-house staff so they become more effective with our systems and guidance.
How often will we interact with your consultants?
Weekly or bi-weekly during the active engagement phase, plus dedicated monthly review calls. You'll also have WhatsApp and email access for urgent queries between scheduled sessions.
Can we exit the engagement if it's not working for us?
Yes. We offer flexible month-to-month arrangements with a standard 30-day notice. However, 98% of our clients continue long-term once they experience the value of proactive financial management.
